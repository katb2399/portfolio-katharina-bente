Icons

One of our last exercises in the module “Gestaltung I” was to create icons. This was also one of my favourite exercises. Some time passed in this semester and I started to get along quite well with Adobe Illustrator. The assignment was to create icons based on the rules we have during the Covid-19 pandemic.

Based on this I decided which rules are most important for me and designed icons for “at least 1,5m distance”, “wash your hands” and “wear your mask”.

This exercise was also the beginning of me starting to customize common icons for my home screen of my phone. Since then I always change up the colours and background whenever I feel like it to make my home screen look more aesthetic.
