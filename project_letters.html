Design with letters

During the module „Gestaltung I” in my first semester we had to several design related exercises. One of them was to use different letters and numbers to design several motives. The first design, which you can see on the right, I created was a butterfly made with the letters U,L, I and J. My second design which you can see on the left was a motive that reminds me of a letter box. For this, I used the letters D,T and K. For this week our assignment was to stick to the colors black and white and if we want to include colours we can use black and any colour we want.

For the next exercise we were able to use colours. Our assignment was to apply different contrasts, which we learned about a few lectures before, on our favourite designs from the last exercise. My favourite designs were the butterfly and the letter box so I used them. On the left at the bottom you can see the quantity contrast applied on the letter box. If you think of the white as something transparent you can clearly see that the berry colour dominates the design but still it harmonizes very good with the blue. On the right at the bottom you can see the cold-warm contrast applied on the butterfly. The reason I choose these colours is that I think they harmonize really good with each other and give off a really rich or expensive vibe.
