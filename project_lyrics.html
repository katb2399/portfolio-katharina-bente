This assignment was one of the many tasks we had in the module "Gestaltung I" in our first semester. We had to choose different lyrics and use different colors and fonts, also font weight and font size, to deliver a message.

I would say this was one of the hardest tasks for me. And I also had to do a lot of research. I wanted to use colours that help me deliver the message I had in mind for each lyrics. The easist one was the lyrics from ,,Flicker" from Niall Horan. I had this idea in my head from the start and I was so happy it worked out the way I wanted. Before I did the exercise I tried to do some research on what colour gives off which vibe. I also looked up the meaning behind the lyrics to understand what the artist wanted to say with these songs. Keeping the artist's meaning in my mind I decided to create the song lyrics with my personal interpretation. Because if someone does not know these songs they might get confused and I believe that the message I wanted to deliver is quite clear.
