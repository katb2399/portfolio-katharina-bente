Billboard campaign

This is also one of the exercises from the module “Gestaltung I”. This was definitely one of my favourite assignments. I had a lot of fun doing this! I had a small idea in my head and also a word joke I really wanted to use. Based on these two things I thought of a marketing campaign and a billboard. It took me some attempts to get it the way I wanted because the billboard has a little add on I think I never saw before.

Since the product I created this billboard campaign for are cookies made in cooperation with the Sesame Street I used items everyone who knows sesame street is able to recognize like cookie monster’s eyes or the blue of his fur which I used for the background. My little add on was the bitten of edge in the top corner. Apart from the billboard I also wanted to see if my poster also works at a bus station which is how I ended up with another design.
